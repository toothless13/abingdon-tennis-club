Welcome to Abingdon Tennis Club

About Us

Whether you are new to tennis or have been playing for years,...enjoy competition or just wish to play socially - you will be very welcome at Abingdon Lawn Tennis Club.

Try us out without obligation by coming along all year round from 6.00pm on Tuesday or Thursday evenings or 10am on the first Sunday of each month. Find out about membership options on the Membership tab above or email us with any queries.

Coaching

Coaching at the club is undertaken by Bart Wisniewski, Craig Amison-Smith and Bev Woodcock. Bart and Craig will be the main club coaches for the junior coaching programme. Together they also run coaching sessions for adult improvers and advanced players.

Wednesday coaching with Bart and Craig for juniors age 5 to 18 will resume on Wednesday 7th September 2022 This year we are adding a second junior coaching session on a Monday afternoon from 12th September 2022 from 3.30pm to 6.30pm. Please contact Craig Amison-Smith if you are interested any junior coaching

Bev is running his ever popular beginners and improvers Friday evening classes. These classes continue over the summer into winter on a pay and play basis. If you are interested, please contact Bev Woodcock.

Bart, Craig and Bev can be contacted directly for individual coaching.
